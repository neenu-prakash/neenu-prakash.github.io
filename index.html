Background

A deliberate pivot, built on strong foundations

I started my career as an electronics engineer in India, spent six years at State Bank of India analysing customer data, translating regulatory requirements, and solving complex operational problems — then made a deliberate move into business analysis.

That path gives me something most BAs don't have: I understand technical systems from the inside, I know what it means to serve a customer under pressure, and I've spent years turning messy information into decisions.

Since arriving in Wellington, I've completed a Master of Professional Business Analysis at Victoria University of Wellington, working with organisations including the Department of Internal Affairs, Archives New Zealand, the National Library, and the New Zealand Red Cross.

I'm looking for BA or data analysis roles where my work makes a tangible difference — to the people an organisation serves, and to the organisation itself. I care about doing work that matters, not just work that gets done.
